Why Adopt?

When you adopt a pet you help an animal in need of a loving home and reduce the sales of puppy mills. By adopting not only will you get a greatful loving companion your also saving a life. Unconditional love is as close as your local shelter.

How Pick a Pet can help you?

By utilizing Pick a Pet you help your community and animals in need

Adoption Facts

Approximately 5-9 million animals are euthanized (60% of dogs and 70% of cats). The number of euthanized animals could be reduced dramaticly if more people adopt pets instead of buying them.

About Us

Pick a Pet is a free and easy way to find the next member of your family. Our mission is to help you along the way and make it as convenient as possible to to find your furry companion. We list dogs and cats that are available locally in your area and are ready to adopt
