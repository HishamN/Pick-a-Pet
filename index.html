Why Adopt?

When you adopt a pet you help an animal in need of a loving home and reduce the sales of puppy mills. By adopting not only will you get a greatful loving companion your also saving a life. Unconditional love is as close as your local shelter.

How Pick a Pet can help you?

By utilizing Pick a Pet you help your community and animals in need. You can choose a pet that is compatible to your family and its needs. All animals that are submitted to shelters have been evaluated and vaccinated to ensure the best possible chance for there new home.

Adoption Facts

Approximately 5-9 million animals are euthanized each year (60% being dogs and 70% being cats). The number of euthanized animals could be reduced dramaticly if more people adopted pets instead of buying them. Also adopting a cat or dog cost less than buying one from a pet store.

About Us

Pick a Pet is a free and easy site to find the next member of your family. Our mission is to help you along the way and make it as convenient as possible to find your furry companion. We list dogs and cats that are available locally in your area and are ready to adopt. Our goal is to educate people about the horrible truth behind puppy mills and find homeless pets a forever home. Because every animal deserves to be loved.
